Microbial Mayhem Privacy Policy

Effective July 16, 2026.

Microbial Mayhem is designed to work offline and does not require an account. The game does not collect, sell, or share personal information and does not use advertising or cross-app tracking.

Information stored on your device

Game settings, favorites, discovered traits, match history, tutorial state, and an interrupted-match snapshot are stored locally on your device. Removing the app or clearing its storage removes this information.

Optional internet access

The game can check for an updated scientific catalog when you explicitly request it. A failed update never blocks play and the last validated local catalog remains active. This request does not include an account identifier, precise location, contacts, photos, health information, or advertising identifier.

Children

The game does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Scientific sources

Scientific catalog records are derived from developer-retained BacDive data with MIBiG enrichment. These are organism records, not player data.

Changes

If the game’s data practices change, this policy and the store privacy disclosures must be updated before that version is released.
